# Why the Name KDNA

KDNA is a metaphor for **Knowledge DNA**: selected judgment can be encoded in a
structured form, copied without silent mutation, identified by version and
digest, and expressed in more than one compatible Host.

The metaphor does not mean that KDNA contains intelligence, personality, truth,
or an entire mind. It does not make a model stronger. It gives a bounded
judgment system an independent asset and loading contract.

## Why judgment, not only knowledge

Documents and knowledge bases often preserve facts and explanations. Judgment
also includes choices: what matters, which tradeoff to prefer, where a boundary
lies, what risk requires escalation, and when a rule does not apply.

Prompts, Skills, Policies, Memory, workflows, and ordinary documents can carry
the same judgment. KDNA is useful when that judgment needs an identity and
lifecycle independent from one carrier: version, integrity, provenance,
authorization, projection, replacement, and rollback.

## What can evolve

An author may revise a judgment asset over time. Each revision must be a new,
identifiable version; a Host must not silently replace an attached version.
Evolution is an author or organization decision, not an automatic claim that a
newer judgment is better.

## The mission

KDNA provides an open format and toolchain for people and organizations to
create, carry, inspect, authorize, and use their own bounded judgment assets
across compatible AI systems.
